## Description

What's your Brain Score?

Memori is a science-backed cognitive training app that measures and improves your memory, speed, and visual processing. Take the Brain Score assessment, challenge friends, and watch your brain get sharper every day.

YOUR BRAIN SCORE
Take a 3-minute assessment that tests digit span, reaction time, and visual memory. Get your Brain Score, Brain Age, and Brain Type. Share your results and challenge friends to beat you.

SCIENCE-BACKED EXERCISES
- Dual N-Back: The gold standard in working memory training
- Spaced Repetition: SM2 algorithm for long-term memory
- Active Recall: Proven study technique for deep learning
- Memory Palace: Method of Loci used by memory champions
- Chunking Training: Turn random digits into memorable groups
- Mixed Training: Interleaved practice for faster learning

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
- Brain Score history graph showing improvement over time
- Daily streaks with Streak Freezes to protect your progress
- XP and leveling system
- 30+ achievements to unlock
- Weekly brain reports

COMPETE & SHARE
- Game Center leaderboards
- Daily challenges (same for all players)
- Challenge friends with shareable score cards
- TikTok & Instagram-optimized share cards

SMART TRAINING
- Adaptive difficulty that keeps you in the optimal learning zone
- Research-backed strategy tips after every session
- Daily training time limits based on cognitive science (15-20 min sweet spot)
- Personalized exercise recommendations

Built on research from Dresler et al. 2017, Wilson et al. 2019, Rohrer & Taylor 2007, and more.

Download free. Train daily. Get smarter.
